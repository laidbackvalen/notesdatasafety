Data Deletion in Notes

Notes provides users with the ability to manage and delete their personal data directly within the app. Here are the options available for managing and deleting your data:

1. Delete User Information

To delete your personal information (such as your name, phone number, etc.), follow these steps:

  • Open the Notes app and navigate to the settings and account management section.
  • Click on the "Remove User Info" option.
  • Confirm your action, and your user information will be removed from the app.

2. Delete All Notes

To delete all your notes from the app, follow these steps:

  • Open the Notes app and navigate to the settings and account management section.
  • Select the "Remove Data" option from the menu.
  • Confirm your action to permanently remove all notes.

3. Delete Account

To delete your entire account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  • Open the Notes app and navigate to the settings or account management section.
  • Click on the "Delete Account" option.
  • You will be requested to provide your phone number to authenticate if it's you because of the data security
  • Confirm your user credentials
  • Confirm your action, and your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.
